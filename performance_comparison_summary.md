## Key Findings

1. **Insider Advisor Declined Significantly:**
   - Pre: 68.5% win rate (63/27)
   - Post: 52.1% win rate (25/23)
   - Almost 1:1 ratio now (was 2.3:1)

2. **Polygon.io Also Struggling:**
   - Pre: 56.3% win rate
   - Post: 40.5% win rate
   - More losers than gainers now

3. **StockStory Worst Decline:**
   - Pre: 66.7% win rate
   - Post: 33.3% win rate
   - Lost half its performance

4. **New Advisors Underperforming:**
   - Intraday: 33.3% win rate (new advisor)
   - Vunder: 0% win rate (only 2 discoveries, both losers)
   - Flux: 45.5% win rate (new advisor, mediocre)

5. **Discovery Volume:**
   - Pre: 1,247 discoveries across 319 sessions
   - Post: 727 discoveries across 124 sessions
   - Similar discovery rate per session (~3.9 vs ~5.9)

## Potential Causes

1. **Market Conditions Changed** - General market decline or volatility increase
2. **Strategy No Longer Effective** - Market regime shift
3. **New Advisors Diluting Performance** - Intraday, Vunder, Flux added but underperforming
4. **Timing Issues** - Discoveries being made at wrong market cycle points
5. **Exit Strategy Issues** - Sell conditions triggering too early/late

## Recommendations

1. **Review new advisors** - Consider disabling or adjusting Intraday and Vunder
2. **Investigate market conditions** - Check if overall market declined post-Thanksgiving
3. **Analyze Insider advisor** - Review why it declined from 68.5% to 52.1%
4. **Review exit strategies** - Check if sell conditions are appropriate for current market
5. **Consider weighting** - Update advisor weights based on recent performance
